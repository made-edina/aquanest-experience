About Us

AquaNest Experience is a 'one stop' water adventure experience and diving course for all ages (children with parental supervision). Coral Foster Parents experience is one of our leading and unique program.

Empowering Bali's marine realm, we are driven to be the foremost provider of captivating entertainment services. By revitalizing tourism, upliftling locals through employment and skill-building, we over unparalleled water experiences that redifine adventure and wonder.
